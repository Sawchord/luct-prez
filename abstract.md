# A decade in certificate transparency and what may come next

## Abstract 

The Certificate Transparency protocol has been standardized in 2016.


## Description

Certificate transparency (CT) is a protocol designed to provide additional transparency into the operations of certificate authorities (CAs).

In this talk, we recapitulate the last 10 years of it's deployment, in which ways it succeeded, in which ways it did not, and where we can go from here.
